About the Game

One Game. Three Battlefields.

Survival Strike War Zone is a mobile shooter built for non-stop action. Fight alongside AI companions, earn coins from every kill, upgrade your arsenal, and face whatever the war zone throws at you — waves, zombies, or a full story campaign.

New to the game? A dedicated Training Mode launches automatically on your first session — learn weapon controls, movement, and shooting mechanics before you ever step onto the battlefield. No soldier goes in unprepared.

Game Modes

Three Ways to Fight

⚔️

Survival Mode

Endless enemy waves spawn without warning. AI companions fight by your side as opponents flood the battlefield. Every wave is harder than the last — how long can you hold the line?

📖

Story Mode

Progress through a level-by-level campaign with loyal companions beside you. Face increasingly powerful opponents across every mission as the story unfolds and the stakes get higher.

🧟

Zombie Mode

Enter a vast open hill zone overrun by the undead. Zombies spawn from multiple points across the map — track them down and eliminate every threat before they overwhelm you.

Earn & Upgrade

Build Your War Machine

Every enemy you eliminate earns you coins. Spend them wisely — the shop and your companions are waiting.

🪙

Coin System

Earn coins for every kill. The more enemies you take down, the richer your arsenal becomes.

🔫

Weapons Shop

Spend coins to unlock powerful new guns from the in-game store. Always be ready for the next wave.

🤝

Companion Upgrades

Strengthen your AI allies so they fight harder, survive longer, and dominate alongside you.
